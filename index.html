I’m an assistant professor (with a fixed term contract) at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano. I work in the KRDB Research Centre for Knowledge and Data.

Currently, my research focuses on Virtual Knowledge Graphs (VKGs), also known as Ontology-base Data Access (OBDA). My insterests include theoretical investigation, system implementation, and industrial use-cases of VKG/OBDA technology. I am leading the development team of Ontop VKG system, which has been adopted in many academic and industrial projects.
