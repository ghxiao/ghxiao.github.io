I’m a Postdoc researcher at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano. I work in the KRDB Research Centre for Knowledge and Data with Prof. Diego Calvanese.

I am interested in combining theoretical research in KR and implementing reasoning Engines.

I obtained my PhD degree in Vienna University of Technology and my MSc and BSc degree in Peking University.

Research Interests

  • Knowledge Representation
  • Description Logics, Ontology, Semantic Web
  • Nonmontonic Reasoning, Logic Programming, Answer Set Programming
  • Combination of Rules and Ontologies
  • Algorithms and Computational Complexity in AI
  • Reasoner Implementation
  • Common Sense Reasoning, Inconsistency Handling, Inconsistency Measurement
