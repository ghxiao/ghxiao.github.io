I’m an assistant professor (with a fixed term contract) at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano. I work in the KRDB Research Centre for Knowledge and Data with Prof. Diego Calvanese.

In general, I am interested in combining theoretical research and reasoning engines implementation in KR. Currently, my research focuses on Ontology-base Data Access (OBDA). Our team is developing the Ontop system for OBDA which supports W3C recommendations SPARQL, R2RML, and OWL 2 QL.
