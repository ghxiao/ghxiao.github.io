I’m a researcher at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano. I work in the Knowledge Representation meets Databases (KRDB) research group.

I started in October 2009 as a research assistant at the Knowledge-Based Systems Group at Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien), Austria.

I obtained my bachelor's and master's degree in Applied Mathematics at Department of Information Science, School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University.

I am doing my PhD under supervision of Prof. Dr. Thomas Eiter and (until September 2010) of Dr. Stijn Heymans.
